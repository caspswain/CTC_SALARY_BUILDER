🧾 Income Tax Slabs — FY 2025-26 & FY 2026-27 Comparison

✅ Budget 2026 — No Change: Finance Minister kept all income tax slab rates, standard deductions, and rebate limits identical for FY 2026-27 (AY 2027-28) as FY 2025-26 (AY 2026-27). The only key change: Income Tax Act 2025 replaces IT Act 1961 from 1 Apr 2026. Revised ITR filing window extended to 12 months (was 9 months), with a nominal fee for late revised returns.

📌 Key FY 2026-27 Changes (Budget 2026):
• Income Tax Act 2025 replaces IT Act 1961 — effective 1 April 2026
• Revised ITR filing extended to 12 months (was 9 months) from end of tax year, with fee after 9 months
• Fee for revised return: ₹5,000 (income > ₹5L) or ₹1,000 (income ≤ ₹5L)
• Form 15G/15H can now be filed with depositories for dividend/interest TDS relief
• + Health & Education Cess: 4% on income tax | Max surcharge (new regime): 25%
